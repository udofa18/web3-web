What is PhantomX?

Cardano is a groundbreaking proof-of-stake blockchain network, being developed into a decentralized application (DApp) development platform with a multi-asset ledger and verifiable smart contracts. Built with the rigor of high-assurance formal development methods, Cardano aims to achieve the scalability, interoperability, and sustainability needed for real-world applications. Cardano is designed to be the platform of choice for the large-scale, mission-critical DApps that will underpin the economy of the future.

Roadmap

Q1 2022

  1. Improve the user experience for first-time Sellers on the AnyTask™ Platform
  2. Build upon the ETN-Network instant payment system to enable a more seamless integration for third-parties
  3. Collaborate with other blockchain projects who may choose to offer ETN as reward to their users

Q2 2022

  1. Expand the choice of products and services within the ETN App
  2. Integrate food, building materials, retail vouchers, and much more into the ETN App
  3. Expand existing services to more regions, whilst lowering costs of existing services
  4. Team up with other wallets and providers

Q3 2022

  1. Pursuit of listings on leading exchanges
  2. Pursuit of FIAT pairings on exchanges that can support local ecosystems
  3. Continue lobbying governments on crypto legislation, as members of the Digital Pound Foundation, CryptoUK and Blockchain for Europe
